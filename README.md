# RemindPay 💸

RemindPay is an Android app that helps you track subscriptions, recurring payments, and debts in one place.

It allows users to monitor monthly expenses, receive reminders for upcoming payments, and keep a history of transactions to stay financially organized.

Works with Android 6 or above.
---

## 📱 Features

-  Payment reminders (notifications)
-  Subscription & recurring payment tracking
-  Debt & installment management
-  Monthly total overview for subscriptions.
-  Payment history tracking
-  Multi-language support (English / Spanish)
-  Multi-currency support (USD + NIO)
-  Dark and Light theme support
